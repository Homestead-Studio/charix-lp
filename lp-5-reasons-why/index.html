Natural Leather
Charix are made with the finest hand-picked natural leathers which develop a beautiful patina over time. Simply put, this means they become more beautiful the more they’re worn. Typically shoes wear out over time, but natural leather wears in, not out. An added bonus is the soft-calf leather lining which never smells or overheats (perfect for sweaty feet this summer!). There are multiple leather options to suit your taste and style including suede and traditional leather. You feel confident and comfortable walking around in a shoe this beautiful, and your feet will thank you.

These shoes only become more comfortable over time because the memory foam molds to the shape of your foot. Whether walking around town or standing for long periods of time, these will hold up beautifully and keep your feet supported.

The versatility that natural leather shoes provide means they can be worn with casual and formal attire. Sustainable
