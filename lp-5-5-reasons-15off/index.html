5 Reasons Why Charix Shoes Are Warmer weather ready!

It may not feel like it now, but warmer weather is finally within reach. And with it comes brand new adventures, experiences, and lovely spring styles.

Below are a few (of the many) reasons Charix are a perfect match for strolling Saturday morning markets, a brisk downtown evening, or a park date in your favorite spring dress.
1
Versatility
While they are worn stylishly with socks during winter months, Charix shine in the spring and summer. Dress them up at Easter brunch, wear them casually during summer travels, or take them dancing all night at your best friend’s wedding. Whether you choose a colorful statement pair or a complimentary neural hue, look stunning wherever you roam.
2
Beauty
Our Ateliers hand-pick only the finest natural leathers. They develop a beautiful patina over time, which means Charix only become MORE beautiful as they age. Typical shoes wear out over time, but natural leather wears in. We offer multiple leather options to suit your taste and style including suede and traditional leather so you can feel confident walking around in your new favorite pair.
3
Comfort
Whether it’s Easter brunch or spending two weeks exploring canals and coffee shops in Venice, you’ll be doing it comfortably in Charix. Each pair features hidden memory foam insoles providing a comfortable fit right out of the box, and only becoming MORE comfy as they mold to your feet over time. So, the more you wear them the comfier they’ll be? You got it.
4
Sustainability
Not only is our leather premium, but it’s sourced by our innovative and sustainable partner tannery in Istanbul. Their tannery is “GOLD-CERTIFIED” by the Leather Working Group, which means Charix represents the highest sustainable standard in the world for sourcing and processing leather. It’s only right that we care about the world we so love exploring.
